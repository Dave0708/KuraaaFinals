About Us

At NextLevelFit, fitness isn’t just a goal it’s a lifestyle. We aim to guide individuals of all levels through powerful transformations, physically and mentally.

Our elite coaches and advanced training programs provide members with a tailored fitness experience that drives results.

  • 📜 History: Built to redefine fitness through discipline, grit, and innovation since 2018.

    • Founded in 2018 by Coach Michael Dave Legaspina, NextLevelFit started in a garage in Quezon City. The goal: to build not just bodies, but lives. It quickly grew into a movement driven by grit, passion, and a relentless commitment to transformation.

  • 💎 Core Values: Integrity, growth, and community support.

    • We believe in doing what's right even when no one is watching. We celebrate growth—both mental and physical—and we’re fueled by the strength of our supportive fitness community.

  • 🧠 Philosophy: Train the mind, fuel the body, elevate the soul.

    • Fitness is holistic. We encourage mental discipline, proper nutrition, and emotional resilience — shaping not just bodies, but character.

  • 🎯 Mission: To empower and inspire a stronger you inside and out.

    • Our mission is to guide people of all ages and backgrounds toward healthier, stronger, more confident lives through intentional training and support.

  • 🚀 Vision: A lifestyle-driven fitness revolution for all ages.

    • We envision a world where fitness is no longer a task but a way of life—energizing families, professionals, and future generations with strength, balance, and passion.
